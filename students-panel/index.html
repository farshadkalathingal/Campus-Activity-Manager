SSM Polytechnic College, Tirur


Seethi Sahib Memorial Polytechnic College is the premier technical institution established in the private sector by Kerala Muslim Eductional Association[KMEA]. Since its establishment in 1962, this institution has made great strides in technical proficiency and set an example for other to follow. The standard of technical education it upholds is unparelleled. The competent faculties of this prestigious institution are the impetus behind these great achievements. The governing body consist of the representatives of the management, State Govt.,and All India Council for Technical Education(AICTE) with the Principal as Ex-officio member.
